Tips to Prevent Drowning

Your safety is our priority. Follow these essential guidelines to maintain a secure pool environment.

Always Supervise Children

Never leave kids unattended near water. Even shallow pools or bathtubs can become dangerous in just seconds.

Learn CPR and Basic Rescue Skills

Knowing how to perform CPR and basic water rescue can mean the difference between life and death before professional help arrives.

Stay Within Safe Swimming Areas

Swim only in designated zones where lifeguards are present. These areas are monitored for safety and free from strong currents or hazards.

Avoid Alcohol Around Water

Alcohol impairs judgment and slows reaction time, making accidents more likely for both swimmers and supervisors.
