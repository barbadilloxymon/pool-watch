Why is Drowning a Major Concern?

Drowning is a serious public health problem around the world, causing about 300,000 deaths every year. According to the World Health Organization (2024), about 29% of global drowning cases occur in swimming pools, making them the third most common place where these accidents happen.

Drowning remains one of the leading causes of accidental death in the Philippines, especially among children.
